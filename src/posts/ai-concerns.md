Advanced AI will reshape work, education, security, creativity, and daily life, quickly and in ways that will be hard to reverse. It may bring real benefits, but it could also introduce serious risks, including the possibility of large-scale harm if powerful systems are deployed before they’re reliably safe and properly governed.

PauseAI builds public understanding and political pressure so decision-makers have the mandate, and the support, to adopt strong governance (e.g., safety evaluations, transparency, oversight, and enforceable limits) and ultimately secure an international agreement to halt dangerous frontier AI development until safety and governance are in place.

We are collecting perspectives from people from all walks of life about your top concerns around AI development, so policymakers and the public understand what’s at stake and can act on clear, practical safeguards like independent safety testing before release, transparency about risks, meaningful oversight, and enforceable rules that prevent reckless deployment.

We know people have different comfort levels with sharing: you can choose whether PauseAI may contact you to clarify details, and whether your story (and any uploaded media) can be used publicly, either with your name, only anonymously, or not at all. If you give permission to follow up, we may reach out to confirm facts, details and consent, so we represent your story accurately.
