## Why this Summit Matters

The India AI Impact Summit 2026 follows previous gatherings at Bletchley Park, Seoul, and Paris. However, this is the first summit held in the Global South, offering a unique opportunity to center the risks that AI poses to developing nations and the need for inclusive, ethical governance.
