## About PauseAI Global

[PauseAI](https://pauseai.info/) is a not-for-profit organisation, active in more than 14 countries. We work to ensure that the development of the most powerful AI systems is safe and democratically controlled. We do this by informing the public, engaging with policy-makers and organising campaigns and events worldwide.
